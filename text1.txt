The young prince was also frustrated to see his father sad for him, and he was fed-up with his life.
One night, he crept outside the palace and went to another kingdom.
He started living in a temple, and ate from whatever alms he collected from kind people.
This new country was ruled by a king, who had two young daughters.
They were raised with the best of learning. Every morning they would bow at their father's feet to seek his blessings.
The comments of the second daughter made the king very angry and one day, he summoned his ministers, "Take her and marry"
